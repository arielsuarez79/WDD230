How to Become a Member

We would be overjoyed to have you join our business community!

Becoming a member of the Tucuman Chamber is simple. Just fill out our member registration form and Chamber staff will follow up with you. Our memberships run on a calendar year, January through December, so depending on the month you join, you will pay a prorated rate. Our staff will discuss the options with you. You also have an option to pay a monthly

Non Profit Membership ($100)

Best for solopreneurs and professionals seeking networking access to Chamber programming. One representative is registered as a Chamber member. Join at this level if you want to participate in Chamber activities but don’t need to promote your business via our marketing programs.

Bronze Membership ($200)

Best for small to medium-sized businesses seeking Chamber access AND marketing and promotional exposure. Join at this level if you want to use Chamber marketing programs to promote your business. Up to three representatives or employees may be registered as members.

Silver Membership ($400)

Best for medium to large companies and organizations seeking a higher profile opportunity to market and demonstrate investment in the Superior community. Up to five representatives or employees may be registered as members.

Gold Membership ($1000)

Our top sponsorship level providing highest visibility and indication of community investment. Premier access and influence. Up to 10 individuals may be registered as representatives.
